What is Grounding

This thread is about adoration and seeking His face. It's about producing a pleasing fragrance to God. This is the posture where we fall on our knees, put our face to the ground and lift our genuine, revelation-backed worship to our Father. This is what it sounds and looks like. It's marked by a colour. Its output has its roots in scripture. For now this is my attempt at explaining the thread. It's being built for His glory and our joy.
