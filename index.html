The Overseer

We do these sessions with the Holy Spirit. First 30 minutes we worship, pray and make the atmosphere suitable for the Holy Spirit.

Drop all ego, drop all pride, drop all ownership. We are here to receive and steward what He gives.

We meet in our sessions wearing the light of the Word. This means before coming meditate on scripture.

For "The Overseer", meditate on Psalm 135:1-3, Psalm 55:22, Acts 2:25-28.

This song is about ministering to God. When you engage with the song, imagine you are standing in front of His throne and ministering directly to Him.

we start with the pad that gets louder to introduce the atmosphere we are in. then the scripture is spoken. then the praise vocal harness frames the worship. then the vocal solos guide the adoration. we end with the praise vocal harness.
