Grounding
01 · Introduction

What is Grounding

This thread is about adoration and seeking His face. It's about producing a pleasing fragrance to God. This is the posture where we fall on our knees, put our face to the ground and lift our genuine, revelation-backed worship to our Father. This is what it sounds and looks like. It's marked by a colour. Its output has its roots in scripture. For now this is my attempt at explaining the thread. It's being built for His glory and our joy.

02 · Sounds like

Grounding sounds like…

  • Adoration
  • Yearning
  • Soaking
  • Multiple voices in a vacuum, each on their own frequency
  • Minimal sound — no drums
  • Voice dominant
  • Few keys and synths

Righteous
01 · Introduction

What is Righteous

This thread is about working out our righteousness. It's about producing a pleasing fragrance to God through burnt carnality. This is the posture where we endure with joy and love the tension. It's the tension that cuts off the death around us and makes space only for life. In partnership with the only viable partner, the Holy Spirit. This is what it sounds and looks like. It's marked by a colour. Its output has its roots in scripture. For now this is my attempt at explaining the thread. It's being built for His glory and our joy.

02 · Sounds like

Righteous sounds like…

  • Atmosphere with pattern foundation
  • Pattern is drums or strings
  • High rhythm
  • Structure and system
  • Pattern dominant
  • Repetitive
  • Chants
03 · Prays like

Righteous prays like…

  • Submission
  • Provision
  • Forgiveness
